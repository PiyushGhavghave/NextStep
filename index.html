National Education Policy 2020

The National Education Policy 2020 (NEP 2020) aims to transform India's education system by focusing on holistic development, equity, and inclusion. This policy encourages innovation and creativity while promoting critical thinking, laying the foundation for lifelong learning.

The NEP 2020 is aligned with the future goals of NextStep, ensuring students receive quality guidance and opportunities for career growth that are in line with national standards.
